Invitation for Linux Autumn 2014

Tue 14 October 2014

My speaker - break is over. Another major conference on the horizon. This time it's one of the biggest Linux Conferences here in Poland.

I'll talk there about taking next step in automation of Linux containers (Docker based) - cluster management based on Fleet and Kubernetes. Also will tell couple of words about fig and history of scaling webapps.

Still not sure if you should attend? Check this year's agenda and great location of the event! Remember to take a bike (great mtb and road trails there) or running shoes - this is one of the best places to have a workout :)

See you there guys!
