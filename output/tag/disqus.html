For the last couple of years I've been favorizing Open Source software. That's why after many years of using Disqus for comments and Google Analytics for statistics I've decided to go Open Source.

Why?

  • Because it's open source
  • Because I like hosting my services on my servers
  • Because I value my privacy
  • Because when I host something then it's on my rules
